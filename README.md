# Mining-Maze-Game
A 2D maze mining game developed following OOP principles.

The game map will look like a maze, where the stone will be the wall, and the dirt will be the path. Within the time limit, player must collect the targeted number of resources, such as 5 diamonds. 
Player needs a tool, such as pickaxe, to mine. Player can also use bomb to bombard the dirt and stone. Player can sell the resource collected to earn money, such as gold, iron and coal. Player can buy advanced tools at the store, such as gold pickaxe and diamond pickaxe, so that he can mine faster. Player has an inventory to store the items, including the resources collected and the tools player has. 
Player’s mining process will be limited by his stamina, each mining process will decrease his stamina. Player can buy food at the store, such as burger, pizza and cola, to regain the stamina by eating the food. Player will have a map to see the structure of the maze-like mine. When player collects any resources, player’s score will increase. The highest score of each game level will be displayed after the game end.
In the menu, there will be three different level, which the difficulty will varies depending on the number of resources, the target diamonds of the game and time limits.
During each round of game, a monster will be spawn. The monster will move towards the player. The monster may attack and reduce player’s stamina or steal player’s item randomly. Player can run away from the monster. The monster will only be spawn after the time pass half of the time limit. After the monster attack or steal player’s item, it will disappear.

To run the game, run Program.cs
